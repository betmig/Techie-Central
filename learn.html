Engineers are the backbone of any tech company. This is why it is so important to have a successful career in this field. There are many different resources that can help you build your career.

The first thing you should do when you want to build a successful software engineering career is take advantage of the free online courses and tutorials available on platforms like Coursera, edX, and Udacity. These courses will provide you with the basic knowledge needed for software engineering as well as valuable skills such as data structures and algorithms.

Another great resource for building your software engineering career is books - they provide more in-depth knowledge on specific topics than online courses do, but they are not interactive, so they may not be the best option if you want to learn something new fast.

In this section, we'll be sharing tutorials, articles, videos a different kind of resources for your benefit.
